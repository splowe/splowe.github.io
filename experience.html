EXPERIENCE

Sound Royalties is a 60-employee specialty finance company that serves music creatives by offering cash advances upfront and receiving royalty payment returns over time. Throughout my summer internship, I worked with both the data management and marketing teams on different company-wide projects.

Data Management

- Edited over 600 customer contacts to provide accurate and additional insights in order to best organize them within Sound Royalty databases to track connections and trends.
Marketing
- Managed social media engagements with the marketing team, and created social media/blog content.
- Created new documentation of company processes by using SalesForce Sandbox (CRM) in order to streamline communication within the sales department.
